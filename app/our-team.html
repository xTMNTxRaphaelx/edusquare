ER. R.K. GOYAL (DIRECTOR)

A man with a vision to make quality education easily available to all students who have the willingness, Er. R. K. Goyal is a bundle of positive energy. His undying zeal to get things done, in the best manner possible is what takes him ahead of the curve. An Electrical Engineer from Thapar University, Masters from IIT Roorkee, MBA from Punjabi University, experience in Punjab State Electricity Board as key personnel and a partner at one of the leading Real Estate group of Punjab: he is a perfect blend of experience, education and vision clarity.

MR. PAWAN KUMAR (DIRECTOR)

A B.Sc. Graduate from Govt. College and Masters in Computer Sciences, he has over 15 years of experience in the coaching industry. He best understands the requirements of the students and makes sure that the institute forms its policies for the benefit of the students. A good eye for detail, clear thinking process and ambitious nature is what makes this happy go lucky person a great leader.

A dynamic young leader, he brings all his team management skills to coordinate Edusquare team to make a meaningful impact on the student’s lives.
